How It Works

No video editing skills required. Our AI handles the complex lip-sync technology — you just bring the content.

01

Upload or Generate a Photo

Upload any photo with a face, or use our built-in Nano Banana AI to generate a photorealistic portrait from a text description. Works with selfies, professional headshots, or AI-created faces.

02

Add Your Audio or Video

Upload an audio file (MP3, WAV) with speech in any language, or provide a video file. Our AI will analyze every phoneme, tone, and timing nuance.

03

Get Your Lip-Synced Video

Our AI generates a perfectly synchronized video in under 2 minutes. Natural jaw movements, accurate visemes, and seamless blending — ready to download and share.
